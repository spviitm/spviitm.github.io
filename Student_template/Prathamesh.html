About

Period: 2025 (Aug) - Present
Project type: PA-1

I am currently working as a Project Associate in the Department of Civil Engineering at IIT Madras, where my research focuses on reservoir monitoring and prediction through the integration of satellite remote sensing and advanced deep learning techniques. I completed my M.Tech in Geoinformatics from NIT (National Institute of Technology) Surathkal, Karntaka, where I worked on paddy field mapping and yield estimation as well as land use and land cover change detection using satellite data and machine learning.

My academic and research interests lie in the fields of water resources, agriculture, and environmental monitoring, with a broader goal of applying GIS, Remote Sensing, and AI-based approaches for sustainable resource management.
