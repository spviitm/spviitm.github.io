About

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Environmental Engineering from Ardhi University (Tanzania) and am currently pursuing a Master of Technology (M.Tech) in Hydraulics and Water Resources Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras (India). Alongside my studies, I serve as a Tutorial Assistant at Ardhi University.

I have practical experience in plumbing and construction, as well as in Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene (WASH) initiatives through my work with humanitarian NGOs. My professional interests lie at the intersection of engineering, sustainable development, and community resilience.

In addition to my academic and professional pursuits, I volunteer with the Tanzania Red Cross Society (TRC). I enjoy reading scientific and inspirational literature, exploring nature, traveling, engaging in debates, watching documentaries and movies, and staying active through gym workouts and other physical activities.
