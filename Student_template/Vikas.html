About

Batch: 2025 (Aug) - Present
PhD (Jointly guided with Prof. K. P Sudheer)

I am a Ph.D. scholar in the Hydraulics and Water Resources Engineering Division at the Department of Civil Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Madras. My research focuses on hydrological modelling and water resources management, with an emphasis on integrating hydrological models and Agent-Based Models to better represent human–water interactions within a socio-hydrological framework, particularly in agricultural systems. I hold an M.Tech degree from the National Institute of Technology Warangal (NITW), where my dissertation examined the Water–Energy–Food (WEF) nexus in crop production using the SWAT model, highlighting interdependencies across sectors and its relevance to sustainability goals.
